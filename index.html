Yan Zhang

Email: yzhang1@uakron.edu

I am Yan Zhang, an incoming assistant professor in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at The University of Akron.

My research interests lie at Cybersecurity and Privacy Issues in Mobile and Networked Systems, Internet-of-Things, AI/ML-powered Wireless and Mobile Systems, Mobile Sensing, Dynamic Spectrum Sharing, Mobile Crowdsourcing.

I received my PhD degree in Computer Engineering from Arizona State University under supervision of Professor Yanchao Zhang. I received my B.E. degree in Information and Computing Science from Xi'an Jiaotong University, China, in 2014 and M.E. degree in Communication and Information System from Beijing Normal University, China, in 2017.

[Google Scholar]


My group has multiple positions for PhD student and Visiting students. If you are interested, please send me an email with your CV, and publications (if any).
