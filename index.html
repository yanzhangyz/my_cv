    Research

    • My research interests include cybersecurity and privacy issues in mobile and networked systems, Internet-of-Things (IoT), AI/ML-powered wireless and mobile systems, emerging wireless networks, mobile sensing, dynamic spectrum sharing, mobile crowdsourcing, cyber-physical systems, etc. My overall goals are to make the networked systems resilient to a variety of attacks by investigating the vulnerabilities in the systems, protect the sensitive information of system devices and participants, and promote security and privacy by design.

      • Current research:

    • User and device authentication in IoT systems
    • AI/ML-powered security and privacy solutions in large-scale IoT sensing systems
    • Security and privacy in UHF RFID systems
    • Attacks and defenses in cross-technology wireless networks
    • AI/ML-powered attacks and defenses in wireless and mobile systems
    • Secure IoT communications for smart cities
    • Secure and privacy-preserving edge/cloud computing in mobile and IoT sensing systems
